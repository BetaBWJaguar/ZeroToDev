Subtracting a coupon or promotional discount from the bill—Discounts—is managed using minus-equals (-=). Adding tax or a tip by calculating a percentage—Tax & Tip—is represented by times-equals (*=); for instance, multiplying the total by 1.25 instantly adds a 25% increase to the bill. Finally, dividing this bill fairly among friends at the table—Fair Split—is achieved using the division (/) operator.
